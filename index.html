About App

An application made based on anxiety and high expectations regarding comfort when carrying out activities in the library.

A mobile-based library application was created to increase library visitors efficiency and convenience.

  • Description
  • Concept
  • Business Model

The library is an institution that collects printed knowledge and recorded, managing in a unique way to meet the intellectual needs of the students its use through a variety of knowledge interactions. The role of the library is as a science center that can give birth to ideas and creativity, in addition to the library also as a community empowerment center to develop based potential liberal.

The application that we will make is the SaveLib application which is for convenience transactions in the library. This application consists of several features, including book lists, borrow books, features visiting, return feature, repository thesis feature, quiz feature, and pay the fine feature if it's late return books.

The concept that is presented and superior to this application is the Gamification concept.

Gamification describes transferring game-like elements into a context outside the actual game. Game design elements and processes are commonly used in different contexts with gamification. The goal is to effect changes in user behavior.

The goal of gamification is to provoke changes in user behavior. In marketing, the goal is usually to increase conversions and make a profit. Distinctive game design elements have also been used in the industry. Therefore, gamification is often about increasing employee motivation which is very important in a monotonous job.

The business model implemented in this application is the membership business model.

A Subscription Business Model or SCM is a business model where customers are expected to pay recurring fees to gain access to certain products or services on an ongoing basis. SCM can be differentiated from other business models based on their primary focus, namely value, predictability, and customer relationships. This business model generates revenue through customer payments for long-term access to the company's goods or services. The main focus of SCM is value, predictability, and customer relationships.

App Features

SaveLib offers several features to enhance your visit to the library.

  • Book List

  • Borrow

  • Return

  • Pay Fine

  • Quizz

  • Repository

Book List

SaveLib can display a series of lists of books contained in the library. Starting from various kinds of genres, titles, and so on.

  • There is a search feature that makes it easier for us to find the book we are looking for.
  • There is a filter feature based on the category of books we want to search for.
  • A brief description of the synopsis of the book you are looking for, along with the author and year of publication, is also provided.

Borrow

The book lending feature offered by SaveLib makes it relatively easy for visitors. Visitors do not need to look for the book they want to borrow on the shelf provided but can search for the book's title via the book list feature, press the borrow button, and be done.

  • For visitors who are non-membership, the deadline for borrowing books is only three days, and a maximum of 2 books can only be borrowed.
  • For visitors who are membership, the deadline for borrowing books is up to 2 weeks, and the maximum loan is five books.

Return

The book return feature provided by SaveLib is also quite helpful for visitors. Visitors only need to open this feature, and a list of books that have been borrowed will appear. Then visitors only need to return the books borrowed previously to the librarian.

Pay Fine

The acceptable payment feature here will apply to visitors who are late to return the book. Books that pass the borrowing deadline will automatically be recorded as a fine to visitors who borrow the book.

  • The fine that must be paid is 2k/day for each book.
  • Payment of fines can be made through mobile banking, digital wallets, and other cashless payment methods.

Quizz

This feature is one of the two mainstay features offered by SaveLib. This feature provides multiple levels of quizzes from several knowledge areas as well. This quiz can later help users to measure their level of expertise in a field.

  • There are three levels, including basic, intermediate, and advanced.
  • Give badges to users who successfully complete quizz.
  • Providing a learning experience while playing to users.

Repository

The following excellent feature is the repository script. This feature can provide access to users who subscribe to view and read a collection of undergraduate theses that have previously completed their education. This feature can later help us to find thesis references that will be compiled in the final semester.

  • There is a like feature or star for the preferred thesis title.
  • A search feature is available to find the title of the thesis you want to read.
